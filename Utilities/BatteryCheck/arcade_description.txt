BatteryCheck displays battery information from the Thumby Color.

Features:
- Battery level bar graph (the big green bar).
- Battery voltage (digital display).
- Charging indicator (plug icon) - visible when charging, hidden when not.

Controls:
- Left bumper:  temporarily reduce screen brightness (lasts until exit).
- Right bumper:  temporarily increase screen brightness (lasts until exit).
- Menu button:  exit the program.
