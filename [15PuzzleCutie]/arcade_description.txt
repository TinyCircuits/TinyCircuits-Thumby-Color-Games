<< 15 Puzzle Cutie>>

How about a 15-piece puzzle featuring cute illustrations?
I've prepared 8 different designs.
Use the left and right buttons to select a design, then press the A button to start.
Use the up, down, left, and right buttons to slide the panels into place and complete the puzzle.

You can create your own original puzzle by making a 128×128-pixel, 256-color BMP image and replacing the default image with it.
